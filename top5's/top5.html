Top 5 places I would love to visit

The first place Kai would love to visit is Seoul South Korea. Kai loves the culture, food, and nightlife of the city so, it is one of the top destinations on his list. The second place Kai would love to visit is New York City. Kai would love to visit New York to visit family, Central Park, and Times Square. The third place he would love to visit is Osaka Japan. After watching many videos of tourists visiting the city, Kai would love to visit the city for its massive second hard market of rare and vintage clothing, along with trying some of Japan's tastiest street food. The fourth place Kai would love to visit, and a place he is ecstatic to visit soon is Tokyo Japan. Ever since Kai was a kid, he has dreamed of visiting Tokyo to see Shibuya Crossing, visit Tokyo Skytree, and be able to experience Japan's many vending machines! The Fifth destination on Kai’s top destinations List is Sydney Australia. Kai would love to visit Sydney to see Australia’s wildlife, nature, and the Sydney Opera House.


Top 5 places I have been

The first place that Kai visited which he remembers fondly is Dubrovnik Croatia. Kai loved walking through the old city, swimming in the Adriatic, and eating delicious sea food! The second Place Kai loved visiting was Honolulu Hawaii. Kai loved swimming in the crystal blue warm water, exploring downtown Honolulu, and experiencing a Luau. The third place Kai loved visiting was Paris France. He loved going to the top of the Eiffel Tower, Visiting the Louvre, and starting each day with a cappuccino and fresh pastry. The fourth Place Kai loved visiting was Rome Italy. Kai loved visiting the Roman Colosseum, and the Pantheon, and having delicious pasta. The fifth place Kai loved visiting was Zurich Switzerland. Kai loved visiting Zurich’s, Google office, going downtown to go shopping, and eating at Restaurant Zeughauskeller an old armory turned into a restaurant.
